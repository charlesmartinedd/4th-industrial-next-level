Framework

The Evolution of Education

1760s-1840s

Education 1.0

Teacher-centered instruction. Rote memorization. Standardized classrooms for industrial workers.

1870s-1970s

Education 2.0

Mass education expansion. Textbooks and curricula. Specialized subject teaching.

1990s-2010s

Education 3.0

Digital integration. Internet research. Learning management systems.

Now

Education 4.0

AI-powered personalization. Human-machine collaboration. Lifelong adaptive learning.
